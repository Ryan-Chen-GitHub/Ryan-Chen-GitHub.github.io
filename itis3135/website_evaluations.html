Website Evaluations

Bad Website Design: Madewell

Madewell is an Online Website that sells a variety of clothing like Jackets, Dresses, and Jeans. On the surface it may look like a well designed website, however there are some glaring issues that do not follow CRAP principles.

  • Contrast: There are instances where white text is layed over a light background or black text on a dark background
  • Repetition: The repetition on this website is the only part that's good, there is a straight line that goes down the center for many of the different products they have on sale, and it doesn't fail to keep that pattern.
  • Alignment: The alignment seems very off at the bottom of the main page, where the margins don't seem to match on both the left and right sides.
  • Proximity: The proximity seems like it was not taken into account, there are two completely different ways of accessing the men's clothing section and Madewell insider

Good Website Design: Spotify Design

  • Contrast: The color choices are well picked, with black text on light background and white text on dark backgrounds
  • Repetition: This website is very consistent in jumping between banners and tiles to show information toward the user.
  • Alignment: The alignment is perfect in this website, margins are amazing and the alignment is intentionally messed with to add flare in the tile sections
  • Proximity: Each category get their own clear dedicated space on the website, with the most important informatiom closer to the top
