Project Overview

Project Name: Vincente's Shoe Store

Project Overview:

  • Purpose: The purpose of this website is to provide my Client with a website to advertise his Shoe Selling business, as well as help clients know when and how to contact him with any queries they may have. Additionally a browsing catalouge will be available so they can see the products
  • Intended Users: Customers interested in purchasing shoes
  • Overview of Content: There will be 6 pages, the Home page, About me, Schedule, Products, FAQ, and a Contact page which all help the customer know everything they need to know to make a purchase

Site Map:

Page Design:

  • Home Page (What):
    • Name:Home Page
    • Purpose:Explaining the purpose of this website, and what it does
    • Audience:Customers
    • Content:This page will give an overall summary of what this website is about, as well as what services and information the website provides.
  • About Me (Who):
    • Name: About me
    • Purpose: Explaining my client and their business
    • Audience: Customers
    • Content: This page will contain a few paragraphs explaining my client's experience, education, and business. This will also go into how this business works and how the community benefits from this. Since this is an about me page, there will most likely not be anything here needing validation or hyperlinks, but will be added if needed
  • Schedule (When):
    • Name: Schedule
    • Purpose: Showing when my Client is available to make a sale or negotiate pricing
    • Audience: Customers
    • Content: This page will contain a few paragraphs stating when my client is available for discussion and what is necessary to be discussed when making a purchase such as shipment dates and delivery fees. It may include hyperlinks to certain events that my client will be attending or validations based on the circumstances.
  • Products:
    • Name:Products
    • Purpose: Showing off a portion of the shoes that my client has to sell
    • Audience: Customers
    • Content: This will be the page where my Client can showcase the shoes that they have for sale, including pricing and shipping costs, this may use validation and may also use hyperlinks when attaching other websites for reference. This page will also most likely involve javascript when showcasing the products for sale
  • FAQ:
    • Name: FAQ
    • Purpose: Answering Frequently Asked Questions
    • Audience: Customers
    • Content: This page will involve frequently asked questions that customers have asked in the past, this page will most likely use validation as well as hyperlinks and javascript when answering some of these questions.
  • Contact us (How):
    • Name: Contact
    • Purpose: Offers customers a way to send an email to my client through a form
    • Audience: Customers
    • Content: This page will contain a form including the customers Name, Contact information, and Query among some other things that they can send to my client if they have to contact them for any reason. Submitting the form will turn the contents of the form into an email which will be sent to my client. This website will use javascript, datafields, and validation to make sure the page is running smoothly.

Dynamic Functionality

Although things might change, here is what I am currently planning

  • Photo gallery where users can view the different shoes on sale (Products).
  • Form where users can submit queries to the client (Contact) example would be how several images in one post work on Instagram.
  • A way for customers to filter for shoes they are interested in based on price and preference (Products).
