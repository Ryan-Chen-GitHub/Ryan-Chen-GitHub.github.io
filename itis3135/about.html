About

The Rare Capybara is a rental company that allows you to rent out a capybara for any occasion, whether it is for a Marriage, Birthday, or even Housewarming Party!

When renting out a Capybara, there are many snacks, toys, and outfits for you to purchase or rent as well to play with the Capybara. We hope to share the joy of Capybaras with everyone.
